## Resources
ICE Portal
This website is for any alien who has been placed in removal proceedings and contains important information for aliens to complete necessary tasks related to the immigration process such as:
- Updating Address with ICE– Expedite changes of address for aliens and information online to comply with immigration obligations.
- Court Hearing Details– View your current court hearing details. Your court hearing is the chance for you to explain why you believe you are eligible for relief or protection from removal.
- Scheduling or Changing Appointments– Check in with your local ICE office.
Online Detainee Locator System
Locate someone in immigration detention
ICE Case Review Process
Enables aliens who believe they should not be subject to civil immigration enforcement to have their case reviewed by an ERO Senior Reviewing Official
ICE ERO Detention Reporting and Information Line
Toll-free number for detainees and community members to raise detention-related questions and concerns to ICE ERO
Victims Engagement and Services Line (VESL)
Report sexual or physical assault, abuse, mistreatment or human trafficking while in ICE detention: 1-833-383-1465
Attorney Information and Resources
