## Careers at USCIS
At U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS), our diverse and dedicated workforce upholds America’s promise as a nation of welcome and possibility with fairness, integrity, and respect for all we serve. Each day, our work impacts the lives of millions of people around the globe seeking family connection, humanitarian protection, and employment and business opportunities. You can make a difference in the lives of so many and help us build a stronger, more inclusive America for generations to come. Search for USCIS jobs at USAJOBS .
Career Opportunities USCIS offers many exciting career opportunities to make a meaningful impact on our country’s future. Find your fit in the USCIS team and transform your career. How to Apply There are many opportunities to join U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS) and begin or continue a meaningful career. Our job opportunity announcements are posted on USAJOBS ; check out our job opportunities and review the information below for additional guidance on how to apply for a job with USCIS. Special Hiring Programs We have specific hiring programs for veterans, people with disabilities, students, recent graduates and other applicants. Benefits At USCIS we put our employees first, offering a wide array of benefits and workplace flexibilities to help ensure our employees’ wellbeing. Training At USCIS, opportunities to grow and develop professionally are available throughout an employee's career.
