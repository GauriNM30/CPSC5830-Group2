## Schools
Designated school officials can use this page to learn about all aspects of Student and Exchange Visitor Program (SEVP) certification to enroll F or M students.
Only schools certified by SEVP can enroll F or M nonimmigrant students. Use the resources below to learn how to become an SEVP-certified school and how to maintain certification.
